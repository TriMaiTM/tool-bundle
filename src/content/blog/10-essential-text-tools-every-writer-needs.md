Whether you're a blogger, student, or professional writer, these free text tools will streamline your writing process and help you produce better content.

## 1. Word Counter

More than just counting words — a good word counter shows:

- **Characters** (with and without spaces)
- **Sentences and paragraphs**
- **Estimated reading time**
- **Speaking time**

Perfect for meeting word limits and estimating content length.

## 2. Case Converter

Quickly convert text between formats:

- UPPER CASE
- lower case
- Title Case
- camelCase
- snake_case
- kebab-case

Great for coding, headings, and formatting consistency.

## 3. Readability Score

Check if your writing is accessible to your target audience. Measures include:

- **Flesch-Kincaid** — most widely used
- **Gunning Fog** — estimates years of education needed
- **Coleman-Liau** — based on characters per word
- **SMOG** — best for health-related content

Aim for a Flesch score of 60-70 for general audiences.

## 4. Text Sorter

Sort lines of text alphabetically, by length, or randomly. Useful for:

- Organizing lists
- Cleaning up data
- Creating randomized orders

## 5. Remove Duplicate Lines

Instantly remove duplicate entries from lists. Supports:

- Case-sensitive or insensitive matching
- Preserving original order
- Showing removal statistics

## 6. Slug Generator

Convert titles into URL-friendly slugs:

- "My Great Blog Post!" → `my-great-blog-post`
- Configurable separator (hyphen, underscore, dot)
- Handles special characters and accents

## 7. Reading Time Calculator

Estimate how long it takes to read or speak your content. Adjust words-per-minute for:

- Average reading speed (200-250 WPM)
- Speed readers (400+ WPM)
- Children's content (100-150 WPM)

## 8. Text Repeater

Quickly repeat text N times with custom separators. Useful for:

- Generating test data
- Creating visual patterns
- Filling placeholder content

## 9. Line Counter

Count total lines, blank lines, and non-blank lines. Essential for:

- Code reviews
- Manuscript formatting
- Data file analysis

## 10. Emoji Picker

Browse and search thousands of emojis with:

- Category browsing
- Keyword search
- One-click copy to clipboard

## Tips for Better Writing Workflows

1. **Start with word count** to set goals
2. **Check readability** after your first draft
3. **Use case converter** for consistent formatting
4. **Remove duplicates** when cleaning lists
5. **Generate slugs** before publishing

## Conclusion

These simple text tools can significantly improve your writing efficiency. The best part? They all run in your browser — no sign-ups, no uploads, completely free.
